Ikhuselo

Ikhuselo is a registered non-profitable organization focused on providing wellness and prevention services to youth in disadvantaged communities.

Mission

Empowering youth in disadvantaged communities through counselling, leadership programs, and wellness initiatives. By addressing mental health challenges, substance abuse, and academic struggles, we create a supportive environment that promotes resilience, well-being, and success.

Vision

To create a future where all youth have access to the support, resources, and opportunities they need to thrive. Ikhuselo Psycho-Social envisions a society where young people are empowered holistically.
